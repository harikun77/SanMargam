Meet the Dancers

Artists bringing SanMargam to life.

Sahana Kumar

A Bharatanatyam dancer and media-arts storyteller, Sahana blends classical technique with contemporary themes. She volunteers with USKids4Water and narrates the feature video.

Sarika Rajan

Sarika's dynamic stage presence reflects years of disciplined training. She contributes to outreach efforts that expand access to arts and education.

Kirthana Warrier

Kirthana brings grace and precision to each piece, with a focus on abhinaya and rhythm. She supports youth education and environmental causes through performance.
